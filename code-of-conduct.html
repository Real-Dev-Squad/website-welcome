Code of conduct at RDS

We are dedicated to building a positive environment that inculcates open learning and high respect for one another. So we expect our members to maintain a certain decorum.

  • No trolling of members on the group. We are here to learn and help each other grow.
  • No spamming off topic stuff. Time is the most important resource. Lets save it in manifolds by reducing off topic content.
  • Avoid Direct Messages. Do not hesitate to ask your queries on the #support discord channel. Aids in quicker response.
  • Avoid using tags @everyone and @here until it is very important.
  • Google your queries before you seek help.
  • When seeking help, submit the code in an easy-to-evaluate fashion. Share a Codepen link instead of a Github link.
  • Overall challenge yourself. Take up tasks proactively and update your progress
  • It helps to have a picture of yourself on your Discord & Github profiles.
